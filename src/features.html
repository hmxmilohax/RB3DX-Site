Features

Rock Band 3 Deluxe improves and expands upon Rock Band 3 in every way possible, like adding much-desired quality-of-life upgrades, and letting YOU get creative and customize the game any way you want!

Here's just a few of the amazing things that await you...

Putting On a Rock Show

Song library too big? Don't know what to pick? Vote for the next tune with 'Play A Show'1, backported from Rock Band 4 to Rock Band 3!

The more players, the merrier!

Meet Some Friends of Mine

We previously said this was impossible. Turns out we were wrong! Pick from over 70 new characters (and counting!) Enjoy watching them be silly on-stage with the 'Custom Characters' build.

They also sync online for anyone else with the build!

Express Yourself

Make the game yours by picking from a TON of options to customize your interface. Change anything from your gems, the length and angle of your highway, individual fret colors, to the highway background, and even the HUD.

Move things around at your whim and share your themes with others!

No Guitar? No Problem!

Join in with standard gamepad controllers, or keyboard if you're on emulator! Simply select a song like you normally would on vocals, and you'll see extra parts available.

You can also remap the buttons to your preference and even change slots to earn achivements for guitars, drums, and keys!

Not The Same Old Song

Want to play that keyboard part from Rock Band 1 that you couldn't before? Maybe you want a duet of "Bring Me to Life" with someone.

Enjoy retroactive song upgrades, with new harmony parts, keyboards, and even Pro Guitar support!

Overhauled Online

Rock Band 3's online is back thanks to GoCentral2! Jump in with 4 different instruments, swap parts without leaving a setlist, taunt your friends with Quick Chat, and other fun stuff!

With nearly 6000 users, and 1.7 million scores submitted, the online experience has been overhauled to be better than ever!

Perfect Precision

Hone your skills with detailed judgements, mid-song calibration, and even accuracy/average stats!

You can also adjust your calibration in increments of 1ms and the "strum" calibration now has a steady rhythm to help you calibrate better!

Modifiers, Modifiers, Modifiers!

Push yourself further with a backported version of 'Brutal Mode'. Enable 'Kick Bounce' to watch the drum track bounce up and down like in Rock Band 1/2. Ignore scores and enjoy the vibes in Karaoke Mode.

These and many other modifiers are available to tweak your experience.


In Search Of...

Taking too long to scroll to that one song you want? Plug up a keyboard and find things easily in your library.

It even works with the Xbox 360 Chatpad and on RPCS3!


Backwards Compatible

Need to uninstall Deluxe to record some FUBs for some old site? Your saves are backwards compatible3, should you ever decide to return to Rock Band 3 vanilla.
